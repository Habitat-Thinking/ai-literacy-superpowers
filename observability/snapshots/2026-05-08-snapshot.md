## Notes

- **Disposition vocabulary inconsistency in objection records.** The
  diaboli's disposition field uses different vocabularies across
  records: `accept` (some records) vs `accepted` (others), `fix` /
  `rejected` / `moot` / `clarify` / `leave` mixed with the
  spec's nominal `accepted` / `deferred` / `rejected` triad. The raw
  counts above reflect what's in the records; mapping to the format
  spec's three buckets requires editorial judgement (e.g. is `fix`
  → `accepted` or its own category?). Worth a follow-up to align
  authoring conventions before the next snapshot, or to extend the
  format spec to acknowledge the broader vocabulary in use.
- **Severity vocabulary inconsistency.** Records also mix `high/low`
  with `major/minor`. Aggregated with `major≈high, minor≈medium` for
  the headline count.
- **GC effectiveness signal.** Marked productive based on three

  (docs-propagation, reflections-via-PR, label-at-creation). No formal
  /governance-audit run has happened since 2026-04-15, so the metric
  is inferred from constraint additions traceable to recent reflections
  rather than from a periodic GC sweep.
- **Plugin version**: 0.35.2 (was 0.19.2 at previous snapshot — 16+
  patch and minor releases in 23 days).
- **Assessment level**: L5 (most recent assessment 2026-04-28).
